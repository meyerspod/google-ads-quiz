Select Your Issue

Rising Cost Per Click

  • Increased Competition More advertisers bidding for the same keywords drives up prices through supply/demand pressure
  • Lower Quality Score Keywords with Quality Scores of 4 vs 6 can cost 16-25% more per click
  • Bid Strategy Changes Smart Bidding strategies may increase bids if predicting conversions
  • New Competitors Entry of new bidders in your market segment
  • Seasonal Demand High-intent search volume in peak seasons increases competition
  • Algorithm Updates Google's updates to auction dynamics or quality scoring
Quick Fix: Improve Quality Score by aligning keywords with ad copy and improving landing page relevance

Dipping Conversion Rate

  • Broken Conversion Tracking Most common cause - tracking tag may not be firing correctly (check DevTools)
  • Website Changes Recent updates to checkout flow, forms, or landing page structure
  • Poor Landing Page Experience Slow load time, unclear CTA, or mobile responsiveness issues
  • Campaign Setting Changes Changes to bidding strategy, budget, keywords, or audience targeting
  • Quality Score Decline Poor ad relevance or expected CTR attracting wrong audience
  • Audience Saturation Retargeting audience exhaustion or diminishing returns
First Step: Compare Google Ads conversions with GA4 - if only Ads dropped, it's likely a tracking issue

Low Traffic / Impressions

  • Insufficient Budget Daily budget too low or reaching daily cap too early
  • Ad Disapproval Ads may be paused or not serving due to policy violations
  • Poor Keyword Relevance Keywords not matching user search intent or too narrow
  • Low Quality Score Low ad rank reduces impressions and placement positions
  • Restrictive Targeting Audience, location, or device targeting too narrow
  • Search Volume Issues Keywords with low search volume or off-season decline
Quick Check: Use Google Ads' Diagnosis tool to identify why specific search terms aren't serving

Low Quality Score

  • Poor Ad Relevance Ad copy doesn't closely match search query or keyword intent
  • Low Expected CTR Ad position, messaging, or formatting not compelling to users
  • Poor Landing Page Experience Page doesn't match ad messaging, slow load time, or poor mobile optimization
  • Overly Broad Keywords Broad match keywords triggering for irrelevant searches
  • Disorganized Ad Groups Too many keywords with different intents in one ad group
  • New Keywords Keywords with insufficient impression history need time to gather data
Impact: Quality Score of 6 vs 4 = 16-25% higher CPC. Quality Score of 10 = up to 50% lower CPC than average

Ads Disapproved

  • Policy Violations Ad content violates Google Ads policies (prohibited content, misleading claims)
  • Landing Page Issues Destination URL is broken, has malware, or violates policies
  • Unrelated Keywords Ad copy and destination don't relate to keywords in ad group
  • Invalid Character Use Special characters or formatting that violates ad policies
  • Trademark Violations Using trademarked terms without authorization in certain contexts
  • Geographic Restrictions Ad content not compliant with geo-specific regulations
Resolution: Click "View and fix policy issues" in the disapproval notice, then read the specific policy link

Low Click-Through Rate (CTR)

  • Weak Ad Copy Headline and description lack urgency, clarity, or compelling value proposition
  • Poor Ad Position Ad showing at lower positions due to low Quality Score or bid
  • Missing Ad Extensions Not using callout, sitelink, or structured snippet extensions to stand out
  • Irrelevant Keywords Keywords matching non-qualified searches not matching user intent
  • Lack of Differentiation Ad doesn't stand out from competitors in search results
  • Mobile Optimization Issues Ad text or destination page not optimized for mobile users
Quick Fix: Add more ad extensions, improve headlines with keywords, and test variations to improve CTR
